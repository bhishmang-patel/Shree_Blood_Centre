Our Services

Comprehensive blood banking services available 24/7.

Blood Donation

Whole Blood, PCV, FFP and Platelets available with safe care and quality service.

Blood Testing

Comprehensive blood testing including blood typing, disease screening, and quality assurance using Elisa method.

Emergency Supply

24/7 emergency blood supply service for hospitals and medical facilities across the region.

Component Separation

Advanced blood component separation for platelets, plasma, and red blood cell concentrates.

Donor Camps

Organized blood donation camps at schools, colleges, corporate offices, companies and community centers.

About Shree Voluntary Blood Centre

Our History

Established in 2022, Shree Voluntary Blood Centre has been serving Himatnagar and Gujarat community with dedication. We have successfully collected over 50,000 units of blood and saved countless lives.

Our Mission

To ensure safe, adequate, and timely supply of blood and blood products to all patients in need, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Certifications

Licensed by Gujarat State Council for Blood Transfusion and fully authorized to collect, screen, and distribute blood products.
